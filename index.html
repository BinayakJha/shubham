Kisan Nepal App

Kisan Nepal is a mobile application that provides real-time agricultural news and notices to the farmers. Farmers in Nepal lack the privilege of acquiring relevant news efficiently and effectively.

So, the Kisan Nepal app primarily focuses on bringing all the relevant information around the agricultural sector and presents them in one place. To help farmers to keep track of coronavirus cases in their respective districts, the easily accessible interface to display the COVID stats has been added to the app. The farmers are unaware of the privileges they ought to receive from the government. Hence, to bridge this gap, the Kisan Nepal app again brings all the government notices and displays them in a readily usable interface. The app also includes the feature to display the standard rate of vegetables in Nepal. This feature will help them to price their crops and sell them at an appropriate price. Rather than business and monetary purposes, this project's motive is to support the farmers and keep them updated about the agricultural sector. During each course of the project, our ultimate priority has been to serve the farmers solving their problems in the best way we can. The app is free to use. Summarizing the features, Kisan Nepal serves:

- Latest agricultural news
- Real-time coronavirus stats for total cases in Nepal as well as district wise stats.
- Latest agricultural notices issued by the government
- Latest market rates of agro-products.
